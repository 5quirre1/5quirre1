Backrooms
After a therapist's patient disappears into a dimension beyond reality, she must venture into the unknown to save him.

The Backrooms is a movie directed by Kane Parsons (Kane Pixels) featuring people such as Mark Duplass, Chiwetel Ejiofor, and Renate Reinsve.

The Backrooms is a fictional internet horror concept that originated from a 2019 4chan post. It describes an endless, maze-like expanse of empty, liminal rooms that can be entered by "noclipping" out of reality.
It started to become more popular and people started to make wikis, games and the most notable thing is the series made by Kane Pixels.
